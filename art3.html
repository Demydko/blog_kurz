Page Turner

Few authors have left a mark on literature as enduring as Jane Austen. From the witty social commentary of Pride and Prejudice to the subtle, complex characters in Persuasion, Austen’s work continues to resonate with readers two centuries after her death. In this spotlight, we’ll explore what makes Austen’s novels so timeless, how her portrayal of women and society still feels relevant today, and the best ways to dive into her world if you’re new to her work. Whether you’re a longtime Austen fan or just discovering her, there’s always something new to appreciate in her writing.
